What you do

"It doesn't make sense to hire smart people and tell them what to do; we hire smart people so they can tell us what to do." - Steve Jobs

Innovative research and development applied to experiential learning in information technology.

Research
Research and develop new software tools, new technologies, and applications to be used as part of NIU programs as well as the lab.

Serve
Serve as Scrum Master and/or Product Owner to into 4-6 agile student teams. These teams will incorporate many of the agile principles needed to be successful as a modern professional.

Present
Present research findings, demonstrate product development for Lab and NIU student peers, and COB leadership.

Minimum Qualification
Currently enrolled as NIU Student: OM&IS, Computer Science, other majors with OM&IS minor GPA: 3.0

Our most successful students are driven and motivated. They possess the curiosity and determination to drive and sustain a project from start to finish. They are comfortable with ambiguity and embrace change. They have a strong desire to raise the bar, discover challenging questions, and research their paths to the answers.
