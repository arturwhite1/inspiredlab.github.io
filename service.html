How can InspirEd Lab Serve You?

Launch yourself into this immersive learning experience by becoming a servant leader and working with a passionate team of individuals. Available for OM&IS internship credit.

Become a Partner:
Where does this show up?

Act as a partner and let the Inspired Lab team take on your next project! Our team is agile inspired; we believe in the process just as much as the outcome. We look forward to having a strong relationship with you so we can better fulfill your needs, whether they become apparent before our initial meeting or after the latest release.

As a student, you will be responsible for performing unique and innovative research and software development applied to experiential learning in information technology.

Responsibilities

Innovative Research: Student performs unique and innovative research and software development. Ranges from researching and developing in new software tools, researching new technologies that could be leveraged in the future, developing applications to be used as part of the NIU program including student knowledge base and checking into the lab. Collaborates within an agile team: Student will serve as Scrum Master and/or Product Owner to into 4-6 agile student teams. These teams will incorporate many of the agile principles needed to be successful as a modern professional. Present Research Findings: Student will present research findings, demonstrate product development. Creates and presents a presentation for Lab and NIU student peers, and COB leadership. Minimum Qualifications: Currently enrolled as NIU Student: OM&IS, Computer Science, other majors with OM&IS minor GPA: 3.0 (what about MIS students?) All qualified applicants will receive consideration for internship without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, age, protected veteran status, among other things, or as a qualified individual with a disability.

We rise up to the challenge, embrace change, and are energized by working on difficult problems worth solving. We believe in the process as much as the outcome. We are best known for our innovative Agile Classroom approach and our set of open-source adaptable tools for implementing it. We also develop prototypes of tools for reporting, data management, research, and development, often in connection with Northern Illinois University courses.

Approach
Our approach is an immersive learning experience, combining design thinking, lean and agile principles, iterative development, active learning, storytelling, and a healthy dose of experimentation.

Students
Our most successful students are driven and motivated. They possess the curiosity and determination to drive and sustain a project from start to finish. They are comfortable with ambiguity and embrace change. They have a strong desire to raise the bar, discover challenging questions, and research their paths to the answers.

InspirEd Lab is an immersive learning experience where NIU students, faculty, and professionals collaborate at the junction of storytelling, design, and information technology. Taking on challenging projects, we research innovative approaches and develop solutions employing cutting-edge tools and techniques — validating learning along every step of the exploratory path.
