About Us

"Experience is the teacher in the InspirEd classroom" - FB

Vision
to nurture others toward achieving their full potential.
Mission
to develop servant leaders and change agents through a dynamic experiential learning environment where mistakes lead to new insights and learning opportunities.

InspirEd Lab is a community of students, educators, designers, researchers, and developers working on experiments ideated to advance the development of experiential learning in information technology into new spaces. The InspirEd Lab provides an open, collaborative environment for interdisciplinary exploration, where students and professionals learn together as a team.

Learning
Lifelong-learning, the ongoing, voluntary, and self-motivated pursuit of knowledge for either personal or professional reasons. It takes place on an ongoing basis from our daily interactions with others and with our environment. Shapeshifting, constantly adapting and evolving.
Enthuse
Making others excited and interested in the goals of the team. Firing up the enthusiasm chords and inspiring collaborative work.
Attentive
Are the ones who give their undivided attention to the people, problems, celebrations, opportunities, and moments they encounter. They are able to make each situation their most important moment.
Fortitude
Mental and emotional strength enabling courage in the face of adversity. Courage to do what is best and persevere while working on tough problems.

We rise up to the challenge, embrace change, and are energized by working on difficult problems worth solving. We believe in the process as much as the outcome. We are best known for our innovative Agile Classroom approach and our set of open-source adaptable tools for implementing it. We also develop prototypes of tools for reporting, data management, research, and development, often in connection with Northern Illinois University courses.

We believe in challenges

"The brick walls are there for a reason. The brick walls are not there to keep us out. The brick walls are there to give us a chance to show how badly we want something." - Randy Pausch

Approach
Our approach is an immersive learning experience, it combines user experience research, design thinking, lean and agile principles, iterative development, active learning, storytelling, and a healthy dose of experimentation.

Students
Our most successful students are driven and motivated. They possess the curiosity and determination to drive and sustain a project from start to finish. They are comfortable with ambiguity and embrace change. They have a strong desire to raise the bar, discover challenging questions and research their paths to the answers.

InspirEd Lab is an immersive learning experience where NIU students, faculty, and professionals collaborate at the junction of storytelling, design, and information technology.
